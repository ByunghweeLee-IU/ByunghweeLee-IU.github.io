Belief Embeddings with Large Language Models

2026-03-17

Citation#

Lee, Byunghwee, Rachith Aiyappa, Yong-Yeol Ahn, Haewoon Kwak, and Jisun An. 2025. A semantic embedding space based on large language models for modelling human beliefs. Nature Human Behaviour.

Overview#

This paper develops a semantic embedding space based on large language models to model human beliefs and positions in public debate. The project connects representation learning with computational social science and provides a framework for studying relative belief structure at scale.

Why I Think It Matters#

  • It creates a computational representation of beliefs rather than reducing opinion to simple labels
  • It links language model representations to social and political judgment
  • It opens a path toward measuring belief change and persuasion in a structured way
