News Press Landscape and Asymmetric Polarization

2026-03-16

Citation#

Lee, Byunghwee, Hyo-sun Ryu, Jae Kook Lee, Hawoong Jeong, and Beom Jun Kim. 2025. Network analysis reveals news press landscape and asymmetric user polarization. Physica A.

Overview#

This paper studies the structure of the news press landscape and user polarization using network analysis. It reflects my broader interest in media systems, public discourse, and online collective behavior.

Why I Think It Matters#

  • It connects network structure to political and media dynamics
  • It offers a data-driven view of asymmetry in polarization
  • It contributes to my ongoing work on online discourse and social systems
